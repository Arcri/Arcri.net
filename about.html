About Me

I am Arcri, a pixel artist of 3 years and a musician of 6 months.

I started making pixel art as a hobby in late 2018, when I created my first project, a "Terraria" mod known as The Aerovelence Mod. I have made most of the sprites for this mod, and have been touching up on old sprites ever since. I have also created several other projects, several of which are texture packs for Minecraft. I am currently in the process of handling creative design for "In Another Life", a 2D platformer which the player dies and uses the world to pass obstacles as well as traverse maps. Some other projects I have ran and managed include "RunicSky", a Minecraft network.
