In Another Life

In Another Life is a game I started working on with a friend around September of 2021. This will be a 2D platformer where the player travels through 3 worlds, which get increasingly harder. By using the world as an advantage in this dystopian futuresque purgatory, the player will make their way through countless hours of gameplay! We plan to finish this project by Late 2022.

RunicSky

RunicSky is a Minecraft network which started out as a KitPvP server and later grew much more creative, adding new ideas and concepts to the traditional PvP experience. RunicSky is a highly ambitious network that aims to break through the traditional servers, and bring new and unique concepts to the table!
